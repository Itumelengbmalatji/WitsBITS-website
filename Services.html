Workshops

WitsBITS currently offers two levels of formal practical training for REDCap users, and we are in the process of developing practical Bioinformatics courses as well. All workshops are CPD accredited and have a registration process, including an attendance fee.

Introductory Workshops

The introductory workshop is held over 1 day (2 days for the online format) and is a very basic overview of the project design tools available in REDCap. It is intended for those who have never used REDCap before, or those who just need a little bit of extra help to get started.

Advanced Workshops

The advanced workshop is held over 3 days (4 days for the online format) and covers covers more complex functionality within REDCap. This workshop is not for newbies, you need to have mastered the basic design tools as described in the Introductory Workshop Agenda and know your way around the project dashboard before signing up.

Consultation

The WitsBITS team offer consulting services in these broad domains:

  • Expert REDCap project review and design support
  • General health informatics expertise during project planning and conceptualisation
  • Bioinformatics project planning, data analysis & interpretation and pipeline/workflow implementation

The aims of the consultations are to:

  • Ensure that everyone in the area has the opportunity to review the proposed plans and provide their feedback.
  • Build awareness and support for the project through meaningful engagement.
  • Seek feedback on the concept design and identify any issues that should be addressed in the next design stages.

The consultation programme includes both online and face-to-face channels to encourage a broad range of responses from different groups. Consultations are free of charge and are designed to guide and support members of the Wits community with health informatics-related issues. Some projects may require multiple sessions throughout the project lifecycle. For projects that demand more extensive involvement beyond expert guidance, such as REDCap project design, software development, or customised training content, we charge a consulting fee of R739.13 exclusive of vat.
